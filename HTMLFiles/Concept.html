Our Concept

Mission

We are facing a critical time. Although the focus on preserving the climate and the globe for future generations is huge, we still have a long way to go. There is a great need for environmentally friendly solutions and we wish to make a difference!

Denmark is characterized by a flat landscape, which makes cycling a good and environmentally conscious alternative. 15% of all trips in Denmark are by bicycle. Despite a lot of cycling, Denmark's ecological footprint is huge, and if all the people in the world had the same consumption as an average citizen, we would need 4.2 earths. This shows that the search for environmentally friendly solutions is more important than ever, and this are main focus.

We have faith in reuse. We want to support the cycling city of Copenhagen by using the technology to make it even easier to buy used products. We care about the safety of our consumers and have a lot of love for our city. Re:Cycle fights for an even greener bike industry and we hope you do too!

Brand

RE:Cycle is a green brand. We use minimal heavy transport and have a sustainable focus throughout all stages of the business. We also donate 5% of our revenue to the Rainforest Fund, with the goal of making a difference in the world. It is important for us to show that environmentally friendly measures do not come at the expense of quality or you as a consumer. Re: Cycle is a brand that has come to stay.
